Yeezy 500

The principal's tenet is that everyone can afford to wear sneakers.

Sneakers is introduced

The adidas Yeezy 500 is a retro casual shoe created by Kanye West in collaboration with adidas. From the look to the color, it embodies the "daddy shoe" style.The Yeezy 500 shoe body is made of synthetic leather, mesh and suede. It has a retro feeling. The line shape of the shoe body is dynamic and dynamic.With intricate deconstruction, multiple seams, and detailed reflective touches, the Yeezy 500 gives us a whole new retro look.

adidas Yeezy 500是Kanye West与adidas合作共同推出的复古款休闲鞋，无论是从造型还是到配色，都体现出浓浓的“老爹鞋”风格。Yeezy 500的鞋身由合成革、网面和麂皮等多种材质拼接而成，极具复古感，鞋身线条造型动感而富有活力，同时还将adiprene+和天足概念融入到了其中，保证了鞋款的穿着舒适性。复杂的解构，多处的车缝，加上细节的反光点缀，Yeezy 500为我们呈现了全新的复古潮流风范.
